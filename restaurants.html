Restaurants

Magaza Gastro Pub

Magaza Gastro Pub is a new restaurant in Belgrade, with a location on the quay of the marvelous Sava river. It’s main conception is in providing a gastro-oasis of hedonism, and offering a wide variety of dishes from both the traditional and modern cuisine – from the grill, but also from the sea.

Every evening and night bands and singers are in charge of the atmosphere in the big garden, and the enjoyment begins from sunset, which is another amazing trait of this restaurant by the river. The finishing touch is the wide variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, with special attention to over 40 different bottled and draft beers from all around the world. Magaza awaits you at Belgrade’s Beton hall!

Dva Jelena

Restaurant Dva Jelena is located in Skadarlija, bohemian quarter in Belgrade, also known as the Belgrade Montmartre (Montmartre is a part of Paris where the artistic elite of the city gathers for centuries). Dva Jelena is one of the oldest and most popular taverns / restaurant in Belgrade and is also the largest à la carte restaurant, a symbol of tradition and Serbian national cuisine. Best of all, it seems, that every day here you can see smiling people from Belgrade and guests from round the world.

Ambar

Ambar is the first modern, international chain of Balkan cuisine restaurants. Inside its two locations in Belgrade and Washington DC, Ambar blends tradition and heritage with creativity and modern design. The result is an unforgettable visitor experience that reaches all the senses with delicious food, unbelievable drinks and an atmosphere that blends comfort and high style.

Café-restaurant Voulez-Vous

Café-restaurant Voulez-Vous is located in Vracar. Situated in Neimar, close to a park, Voulez-Vous captures you and leaves breathless with its open space, garden component, its brightness, openness and friendly atmosphere. At the second glance it will conquer you with its specialized cuisine and the dishes prepared with devotion and love by our chefs. Altogether you can sense the balance of warmth and friendly atmosphere combined with excellent cuisine and cozy environment
