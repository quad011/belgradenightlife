Shopping

Ušće Shopping Center

Ušće Shopping Center Ušće Shopping Center is the largest and the most modern shopping center in Serbia. It has over 140 stores, a supermarket, restaurants and bars, a food court, multiplex cinema with 11 screens, bowling alley, casino, family entertainment, as well as free parking for over 1300 vehicles.

Open: Mon-Sun 10-22h

Address: Mihajla Pupina 4, 11070 New Belgrade (Novi Beograd)

Delta City Shopping Mall

Delta City Shopping Mall Delta City is a large shopping mall, located in New Belgrade. It contains many stores, cinemas, a bowling alley, a huge children’s playground, coffee shops and restaurants. The International Shopping Mall Association voted Delta City as ‘The Best Shopping Mall in Europe’ in 2009.

Open: Mon-Sun 10-22h

Adress: Jurija Gagarina 16, 11o70 New Belgrade (Novi Beograd)

Knez Mihailova street

Knez Mihailova street This is favourite Belgrade corso, for walking and shopping. Down the way you will find many popular stores and brands. In the end of the corso, just before Kalemegdan, there is a brand new shopping mall called "Rajićeva" and very popular caffes like Boutique 2 and Smokvica, and if you are looking for a great coffee then don't miss Kafeterija or Coffee dream.
