Belgrade New Years Eve 2019:

Best Clubs for Celebrations, Best Places to Visit during holidays and More

Belgrade is one of the best places to celebrate all that was great in 2018. Belgrade has so many NYE parties and club nights, whatever your taste, and it can be quite a challenge finding the perfect new year's eve party for your group. Sit back and enjoy our pick of the very best Belgrade new year's eve parties and events.

The Bank New Years Eve 2019 is one of the most awaited moments by everyone visiting Belgrade during holiday who wants to enter the New Year in VIP style, in premium nightclub, surrounded with beautiful and interesting people, best DJ's playing RnB and house music, and looking for night to remember. Capacity of the Bank club is 400 people, number of VIP and Regular Packages is limited and usualy sold before 1st December.

Freestyler is a legendary Belgrade summer club. During winter they offer closed arena which is popular with mostly younger people - aged 18 - 25 years. Music is mostly house and electro house. Both VIP and Regular packages will be available soon.
